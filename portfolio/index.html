A little about me

Hi, I’m Chase. I am a full-stack web developer with a particular interest in front-end development. My specialties include HTML, CSS, Javascript, React and Node.

***To view my latest projects, please scroll down to the Portfolio section.***

After 6 years in the specialty coffee industry, I’ve developed strong creative problem-solving, organizational, and communication skills. My time as a head roaster has taught me how to self-motivate, continuously improving the end product through careful observation of data as well as the value of being in charge of a pivotal part of a small business. Working in the service industry has given me valuable insight into customer interactions and hospitality. I aim to take those insights and my skills from my years as a head roaster and translate them into creating polished and cutting-edge applications that are seamless and easy to use. When I'm not programming, you can find me rock climbing, walking my corgi, reading as many books as possible or trying a new recipe for my wife and I.

If you’d like to know more, feel free to get in touch!
