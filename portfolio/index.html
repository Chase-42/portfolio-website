A little about me

Hi, I’m Chase. I am a full-stack web developer with a particular interest in front-end development. My specialties include HTML, CSS, Javascript and React.

***To view my latest projects, please scroll down to the Portfolio section.***

After 6 years in the specialty coffee industry, I’ve developed strong creative problem-solving, organizational, and communication skills. My time as a head-roaster has taught me how to self-motivate and be in charge of a pivotal part of a small business. I would like to combine those skills with web technologies to build engaging and cutting-edge projects. When I'm not programming, you can find me rock climbing, walking my corgi, reading a book or trying a new recipe for my wife and I.

If you’d like to know more, feel free to get in touch!
